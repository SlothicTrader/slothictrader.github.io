About this Project

The VAP Deltas study was developed to draw the different between the ask and the bid at each price level on a chart. The Study can be used on a bar chart or a numbers bars chart.

Features

  • Display Regular Delta
  • Display Diagonal Delta
  • Display Digonal Delta and Regular Delta side by side
