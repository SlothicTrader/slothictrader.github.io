About this Project

The VAP Tuner study was developed to easily adjust the "Chart Settings >> Chart Data >> Volume at Price Multiper for Intraday Chart" configuration setting without having to go into the chart settings to do so. The study adds 10 configurable settings to the charts' right-click menu.

Features

  • 10 configurable multiplier options
  • Adds options to the right-click menu of the chart the study is added to
